    Our community outreach, which took place on the first day of February at Ipil St., Marikina, in Saint Maria Matthias, was titled "A Day of Kindness and Compassion." The purpose of this outreach was to bring joy to the children at Saint Maria Matthias for a day. We wanted them to enjoy it and give them time to have a happy day. This outreach was conducted by Grade 9 San Pablo, together with our Araling Panlipunan teacher, Mr. Nano, our Homeroom Adviser, Ms. Villanueva, and Ms. Manalili.
